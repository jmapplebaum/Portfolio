About Me


Hello World! I'm Jennah Applebaum, a passionate front-end developer located in the vibrant city of Tulsa, Oklahoma. My journey into the world of coding began during my time as the Marketing and Communications Manager for a nonprofit organization in Bozeman, Montana. It was there that I discovered my interest in web development and decided to delve deeper.

Fuelled by my innate curiosity and thirst for knowledge, I began my coding journey through SheCodes, where I quickly found myself captivated by the intricacies of front-end development. Drawing from my diverse background in music and education, I love to weave together logic, problem-solving, and creativity to craft compelling digital experiences.
